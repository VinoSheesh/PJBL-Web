Asked Question

What is Crypto?

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency secured by cryptography and powered by decentralized blockchain technology. It allows secure, transparent transactions without the need for banks or central authorities.

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that allows peer-to-peer transactions without the need for a central authority like a bank. It operates on blockchain technology, ensuring secure and transparent transactions.

How to buy Crypto?

To buy crypto, sign up on an exchange, deposit funds, choose your cryptocurrency, and complete the transaction.

What is diversification?

Diversification in cryptocurrency involves spreading investments across different coins or assets to reduce risk and increase potential returns.

What is Memecoin?

A meme coin is a type of cryptocurrency created as a joke or based on internet memes, often with little to no utility, but gaining popularity through social media and community hype.

How to sell Crypto?

To sell crypto on FlyX, log in, select the crypto you want to sell, choose the amount, confirm the transaction, and withdraw the proceeds to your bank or wallet.
